You are undergoing Time Stress.

        You experience time stress when you worry about time, or the lack thereof. You worry about the number of things that you have to do, and you fear that you'll fail to achieve something important. You might feel trapped, unhappy, or even hopeless. Common examples of time stress include worrying about deadlines or rushing to avoid being late for a meeting.

Managing Time Stress

  • Learn good time management skills.
  • Make a To-Do list.
  • Get a planner.
  • Create more time in your day.
  • It's okay to say no to tasks that you don't have time to do!
